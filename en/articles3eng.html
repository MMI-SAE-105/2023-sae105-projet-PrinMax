The Career of Ridley Scott:

Born on 30 November 1937 in South Shields, in England, this director became famous for its exceptional contributions to cinema, notably with the movie "Alien, the 8th Passenger" (1979). Ridley Scott studied at Royal College of Art in London, where he developed his passion for film. He started his career making commercials before making the jump to the cinema.His first feature film, The Duellists (1977), received the prize for best first film at the Cannes Film Festival and was a remarkable introduction to his talent of director.

The influence of Ridley Scott:
